# Moving to the next regime

## General info on start-up relief for businesses

When you first run a business in Poland, you can use several ZUS contribution regimes one after another: first **Ulga na start**, then **Składki preferencyjne**, then **Mały ZUS+** (if you meet the conditions), and finally **Duży ZUS**. Each regime has its own deadlines, switching rules, and reporting codes. Stick to the filing deadlines when you change regime — that way you avoid fines and reporting mistakes.

## Typical entrepreneur lifecycle

Starting point: you’re opening your **first** business in Poland **or** you haven’t run a business for a long time (you closed your previous company more than 60 months ago).

1. With income up to 75% of the minimum wage, no contributions at all (**Działalność nierejestrowa** = unregistered activity).
2. After registration, the first **6 full calendar months** are **Ulga na Start** (6 full months **plus** a partial month if you didn’t open on the 1st).
3. After Ulga na Start ends, you can move to **Składki Preferencyjne** (the next **24 months**).
4. After that, if income is still low, you can use **Mały ZUS+** for **36 months**.
5. When every relief is used up, you must pay **Duży ZUS** in full.

From the “third level” onward, you can also take **one month off ZUS** per year.

## Deadlines when switching between regimes

- The switch always applies **from the 1st of the month**. You have **7 days** to file. If the last day is a weekend or holiday, the deadline moves to the next business day. Don’t leave it to the last minute.
- If you miss the deadline, you can still file later — there’s a small risk of a fine.

📅 Use our [spreadsheet with key dates][3]. Enter your real business start date and get a personalised timeline for free. Then add reminders in your calendar so you don’t miss these dates.

So: start the switch **no earlier than** the 1st of the month you already intend to pay social contributions at the new rate for — but don’t drag your feet, either, so you **finish** the switch **before the 8th**.

## How to switch between ZUS regimes

You can do it online — no need to visit a ZUS office.

First log in to eZUS as described [here][6].

Then pick the step-by-step guide you need and follow it.

**1. Activating Ulga na Start**

- When you register your JDG, register with ZUS using **ZUS ZZA** and pick the right code (**0540 00**).
- More detail: [Ulga na Start — conditions and quirks](zus_ulga_na_start.md)
- Walkthrough: [JDG registration](registration_jdg.md)

**2. Switching to Składki Preferencyjne**

- After 6 months of Ulga na Start, file the switch to the next relief (code **0570 00**).
- Walkthrough: [Switching to Składki Preferencyjne](zus_obnizone_skladki.md)

**3. Switching to Mały ZUS+**

- After the preferential period ends (24 months **preferencyjne**), some of you — those who meet the conditions — can switch to **Mały ZUS+** (code **0590 00**).
- Walkthrough: _TODO_

**4. Switching to Duży ZUS**

- After the relief period ends (24 months **preferencyjne** or 36 months **Mały ZUS+**), switch to **Duży ZUS** (code **0510 00**).
- Walkthrough: [Switching to Duży ZUS](zus_duzy.md)

**5. ZUS “holiday”**

- If you’re no longer on **Ulga na Start**, you can take one month per year without paying social contributions (“holiday”).
- More detail: [ZUS holiday — conditions and walkthrough](zus_vacation.md)

## UPP — proof of submission

**UPP** = **Urzędowe Poświadczenie Przedłożenia** — the proof that ZUS received your declaration, generated by zus.pl. Without a UPP you can’t reliably prove printed **ZUS ZUA** / **ZUS ZZA** forms are authentic.

To prove you’re insured with ZUS, the **Potwierdzenie ubezpieczenia** certificate is usually better. See the [residence permit document checklist][2].

If someone else filed **ZZA**/**ZUA** for you, ask them for the original declaration **and** its UPP.

### If you use eZUS

To get **Urzędowe Poświadczenie Przedłożenia** (UPP) in eZUS (formerly PUE ZUS):

1. Go to [eZUS](https://www.zus.pl/) and sign in.
2. In the main menu open **Dokumenty i wiadomości Korespondencja z ZUS**.
3. Open **Dokumenty wysłane**.
4. Find the declaration and click **Szczegóły**.
5. In the window you can:
    - View the document,
    - Export it to disk,
    - View the submission proof (**Poświadczenie przedłożenia**).
6. To download the UPP, choose **Pokaż poświadczenie przedłożenia** and click **Eksportuj**.
