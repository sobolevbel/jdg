# Ulga na Start

New entrepreneurs can get a ZUS payment benefit: you pay only the health contribution (składka zdrowotna) and you do not pay social contributions (składki społeczne) for the first six full months of running your business.

!!! success "Official site"
    [Ulga na start – 6 miesięcy bez składek na ubezpieczenie społeczne][1]

## How to turn the relief on

You need to enter code **05 40** on the ZUS ZZA declaration when you register with ZUS.

See the step-by-step walkthrough in [JDG registration](registration_jdg.md).

## How long "Ulga na start" lasts

If you start your business on the **first day of a month**, the relief lasts for **six months**, **including** the month you registered.

If you registered **on any other day** of the month, the relief lasts for **six full months** plus the **partial month** when you registered. That split is a legit way to stretch the calendar a bit.

## Who qualifies for Ulga na Start

Per the official rules, you can use Ulga na Start only if **all three** of these are true at the same time:

1. **You are a natural person** — you register a sole proprietorship (JDG) or you are a partner in a civil partnership (spółka cywilna).
2. **You are starting business for the first time** or you resume it **no sooner than 60 months (five years)** after the last suspension or closure.
3. **You are not working for a former employer** — you do not provide services or work under contract for someone you were employed by under an employment contract (umowa o pracę) in the **current or previous calendar year** if those services match what you used to do for them.

!!! warning "Heads up!"
    Pay attention to **condition 2**. If you simply close JDG and open it again with a gap of **1 to 59 months**, you **cannot** use Ulga na Start. You will pay full ZUS.

## Edge-case example

Can you use Ulga na Start if you worked as a tester under an employment contract (UoP) and now you open JDG and do programming for the same employer?

What matters: **your JDG activity must not match what you did under the employment contract**. If you were officially a tester and you now contract for programming, you **formally** stay inside the relief rules.

Polish practice has write-ups of similar cases. For example, if someone was a manager and then became a developer-entrepreneur serving the same employer — relief can still apply if the new activity is different from the old one (see [source][2]).

- [Case write-up: working with a former employer and eligibility for ZUS reliefs][2] (PL)

## Important

- If you start providing services in the **same line of work** as under the employment contract, Ulga na Start does **not** apply.
- If the profile is **different**, relief may be possible — but it helps to have paperwork (scope of services, occupation codes, etc.).

## UPP — proof of submission

UPP = Urzędowe Poświadczenie Przedłożenia — the submission receipt ZUS generates on zus.pl for a filed declaration.

Many people register with ZUS at the same time as JDG on biznes.gov.pl. You can usually get the ZZA file the site generates, but you **cannot** get a UPP file for it. Without UPP you cannot formally prove printed forms are authentic.

So for proving ZUS coverage, people often use the **Potwierdzenie ubezpieczenia** certificate instead. Details in the [residence permit document checklist][3].

If ZUS ZZA was filed **other than** through biznes.gov.pl, ask whoever submitted it for the original declaration and its UPP. More in the [guide][21].
