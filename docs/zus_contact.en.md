# ZUS contacts

📧 email cot@zus.pl  
📝 questions form: [Formularz kontaktowy](https://www.zus.pl/o-zus/kontakt/zadaj-pytanie-zus)  
💻 video visit: [e-Wizyta](https://e-wizyty.zus.pl/) (for JDG-related questions, pick topic "Firmy i pracownicy")

There are known technical limitations for iOS / macOS users; if you use another device, e-Wizyta is a good option. Have your karta pobytu or passport ready—you will need to show the document on camera for identity verification.
