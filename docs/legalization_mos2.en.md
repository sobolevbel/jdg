# Applying for a karta pobytu via MOS

Since 2026, the wniosek (application) for pobyt czasowy is submitted **fully electronically**
via [MOS (Moduł Obsługi Spraw)][1] — the portal of the Urząd do Spraw Cudzoziemców
(Office for Foreigners). You no longer need to send a signed PDF via e-Doręczenia or
by post: the wniosek is filled out, signed via Profil Zaufany, and sent directly on the
portal, and the confirmation of receipt (UPO) arrives in your konto (account).

This guide is based on first-hand submission experience from the community (July 2026).

## Preparation

What to prepare before submitting:

1. **Załączniki** (attachments) — the files attached to the wniosek (except the photo
   and the passport). The number of attachments is limited (originally 19, according to
   the latest information increased to 25+), so it is convenient to merge documents of
   the same type into a single PDF — for example, with the [PDF/JPG merging tool][2]
   by Andrei Mitin.
2. **File size**: up to 50 MB in total, no single file larger than 10 MB. Heavy scans
   will have to be compressed.
3. **Address of residence** during the 12 months **before arriving** in Poland.
4. **Address of residence in your country of citizenship** — in the new wniosek these are separate mandatory fields.
5. **Podstawa prawna** (legal basis) for the application:
    - for a sole proprietor: art. 142 ustawy z dnia 12 grudnia 2013 r. o cudzoziemcach;
    - for the sole proprietor's accompanying family members (inne okoliczności): art. 187 of the same ustawa.
6. **Photo** sized exactly **684 × 883 pixels** (JPG/JPEG). You can fit your photo to
   the requirements with the [photo cropping tool for MOS][3].
7. **Fee payment** (non-refundable): for processing the wniosek and for printing the card.
   Payment details — [information about fees on MOS][4]; for Masovian Voivodeship also
   [Opłaty | Migrant WSC][5].
8. **Passport scan**: all pages, including empty ones and the **covers**, as a single PDF
   up to 10 MB, in color.

!!! tip "Community tools"
    The [JDG tools page][6] by Andrei Mitin has a wniosek generator, an interactive
    document checklist, document templates (business plan, entrepreneur questionnaire,
    income declarations), and a table with voivodeship payment details.

## Registering a konto in MOS

1. Go to [mos.cudzoziemcy.gov.pl][1] and click **ZALOGUJ** in the top right corner.

    ![MOS home page, Zaloguj button][7]

2. The portal will redirect you to Profil Zaufany — log in.
3. After PZ, the konto creation page will open: first name, last name, and PESEL are
   already filled in; you need to add your **citizenship, gender, and e-mail**.

    ![User data form][8]

4. At the bottom, tick the two checkboxes (terms of service and privacy policy) and click **Zatwierdź**.

    ![Consents and the Zatwierdź button][9]

5. A registration confirmation link will arrive by e-mail.

    !!! warning "The link is only active for 15 minutes"
        If you don't see the e-mail — check the Spam folder. Look for the link itself
        in the e-mail under the word **link**.

    ![Activation e-mail notice][10]

    ![E-mail with the activation link][11]

6. Click the link — the portal will open again; agree (**Rozumiem**).
7. A message will appear saying that the konto has been created and you need to log in
   once more: **Przejdź do strony logowania** → Profil Zaufany again → you are in your konto.

    ![Logged-in MOS home page][12]

## Adding a child (konto dziecka)

If you are also submitting a wniosek for a child, first add them to your konto:

1. Go to **Moje konto** → the **Konta dzieci/podopiecznych** section → **Dodaj konto**.

    ![Moje konto, Konta dzieci/podopiecznych][13]

2. Fill in the child's details. Choose creating a **nowe konto** — accounts from the old
   MOS have been deleted.

    ![Adding a child's konto — step 1][14]

    ![Adding a child's konto — step 2][15]

3. Click **Dodaj konto** at the bottom.

## Filling out the wniosek

1. On the home page, select **Krok 3 — Złóż wniosek online** (green).

    ![Krok 3 — Złóż wniosek online][16]

2. Select **Zezwolenie na pobyt czasowy**.

    ![Choosing the wniosek type][17]

3. If there is a child in your profile, a field will appear to choose **who the wniosek
   is for** — select the needed konto.

    ![Choosing whose wniosek it is][18]

4. Next, several screens of oświadczenia (declarations) will appear — read them and tick
   the checkboxes (there should be **5** of them in total), then click **Przejdź dalej**
   at the bottom right.

    ![Oświadczenia — screen 1][19]

    ![Oświadczenia — screen 2][20]

    ![Oświadczenia — screen 3][21]

5. Attach the photo (684 × 883 px, JPG) by clicking the gray **Prześlij fotografię** button.

    ![Attaching the photo][22]

6. Select the voivode (the authority the wniosek is submitted to) and click **Przejdź dalej**.
7. **Dane osobowe (cz. 1)** — all standard: last name, previous last names, first name,
   parents' names. After filling it in, click **Przejdź dalej**.

    ![Dane osobowe — part 1][23]

    ![Dane osobowe — names][24]

    ![Dane osobowe — parents][25]

8. **Dane osobowe (cz. 2)** — date and place of birth, citizenship, marital status,
   education, height, eye color, PESEL, phone, e-mail.

    ![Dane osobowe cz. 2 — screen 1][26]

    ![Dane osobowe cz. 2 — screen 2][27]

    ![Dane osobowe cz. 2 — screen 3][28]

9. **Dokument podróży** — passport details. Pay attention to the explanation about the
   document type (the "?" sign under the field): if you are not a refugee, a diplomat,
   or a sailor, your choice is **PASZPORT ZWYKŁY** (the residence cards in the list are
   not for this case).

    ![Choosing the document type][29]

    A new field — **Liczba osób wpisanych do dokumentu podróży**: fill it in only if
    other people (usually children) are entered into your passport. If nobody is entered —
    tick **Brak**, even if you have children with their own passports.

    ![Liczba osób wpisanych do dokumentu podróży][30]

10. **Adres w kraju pochodzenia** — a new field: your last address in the country of citizenship.

    ![Address in the country of citizenship][31]

11. **Adres w kraju poprzedniego zamieszkania** — the address in the country of previous
    residence during the 12 months **before** moving to Poland. Filled in if the country
    of citizenship and the country of previous residence are different; otherwise tick
    **Nie dotyczy**.

    ![Address of previous residence][32]

12. **Miejsce aktualnego pobytu** — your current address in Poland.

    ![Current stay in Poland][33]

13. **Członkowie rodziny** — relatives in Poland. For a husband/wife the degree of
    kinship is współmałżonek/współmałżonka, for children — syn/córka.

    ![Family members][34]

14. **Główny cel pobytu** — the purpose of stay. For a sole proprietor —
    **prowadzenie działalności gospodarczej**.

    ![Purpose of stay][35]

15. **Poprzednie pobyty** — all previous stays in Poland **over your entire life**.
    If you don't have exact dates, you can describe the period approximately in the
    text field (for example, "od około połowy czerwca do połowy lipca 2022 r.") — then
    the "Data od"/"Data do" fields don't need to be filled in. Dates can be reconstructed
    from passport stamps, hotel bookings, or flight tickets.

    ![Previous stays in Poland][36]

16. **Podróże i pobyty zagraniczne** — trips outside Poland over the last 5 years
    (country, period).

    ![Trips over the last 5 years][37]

17. **Środki finansowe** — means of subsistence and health insurance (see
    [the health insurance certificate instructions](zus.md#health-insurance-certificate)).

    ![Financial means and health insurance][38]

18. **Criminal record questionnaire** — questions about convictions and restrictions.
    This data is sent directly to the registers and is not stored in MOS: you will not
    see it in the wniosek preview or in the draft version.

    ![Criminal record questionnaire][39]

19. **Uzasadnienie wniosku** — the podstawa prawna is entered **manually** (there is no
    drop-down list): for a sole proprietor — `art. 142 ustawy z dnia 12 grudnia 2013 r. o cudzoziemcach`.

    ![Uzasadnienie wniosku][40]

20. Final oświadczenia.

    ![Final oświadczenie][41]

21. **Skan dokumentu podróży** — the passport scan as a single PDF (up to 10 MB): all
    pages, including empty ones, **and the covers**, in color, of readable quality.

    ![Passport scan][42]

22. **Załączniki** — attachments to the wniosek (JPG or PDF, one file up to 10 MB,
    everything together up to 50 MB). The composition of the document package — in the
    [checklist below](#interactive-document-checklist).

    ![Załączniki screen][43]

    The załączniki are numbered **down the left side** of the list.

    ![Numbered list of załączniki][44]

## Signing and sending

1. At the bottom of the załączniki screen, the buttons **Zapisz wersję roboczą** (save
   a draft) and **Podpisz przez login.gov.pl i wyślij** will appear — click the second one.
2. On the "Sprawdź dokumenty przed podpisaniem" screen, click **Podpisz**.

    ![Checking the documents before signing][45]

3. The portal will redirect you to signing via Profil Zaufany **several times** — sign
   every time.
4. After signing, the message **Wniosek został wysłany** will appear with the buttons
   **Pobierz PDF / Pobierz UPO / Pobierz podpisany XML**.

    ![Wniosek sent][46]

5. Wait until the **UPO** (urzędowe poświadczenie odbioru — official confirmation of
   receipt) appears, and **download the wniosek as a PDF right away** — you will be asked
   to bring the printed wniosek to the wezwanie (the fingerprints appointment).

!!! info "What's next"
    The UPO is proof that the voivode has received your wniosek. After the formal check,
    the voivode will generate a zaświadczenie (certificate) in MOS confirming the legality
    of your stay while the case is being processed. Then wait for the fingerprints
    appointment (the invitation arrives by e-mail) and bring the **complete document
    package** to it (do not hand over the originals — the urząd (office) does not return
    anything) and the printed wniosek.

## Timelines

- Fingerprints appointment: approximately **~6 months** after submission (Masovian Voivodeship).

!!! note "Alternative voivodeships"
    If you are going to apply for citizenship or long-term residency, it is worth
    considering other voivodeships: **Lodz** (a couple of months of waiting),
    **Poznan** (up to four months).
