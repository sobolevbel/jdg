# ZUS VACATION: Everything Entrepreneurs in Poland Need to Know

**Starting from November 1, 2024, sole proprietors in Poland (JDG) can
take advantage of the so-called "ZUS vacation" ("Wakacje od ZUS")**,
meaning they can get an exemption from paying social contributions ("składki społeczne")
for one month per year.

First and foremost, this new benefit should interest entrepreneurs who pay Duży ZUS.
If your Ulga na Start hasn't ended yet, you can skip the details for now and come back to this topic later.

## Eligibility for ZUS Vacation

To receive the exemption, you must meet the following requirements:

- During the month preceding the application month, the entrepreneur must have been insured in ZUS for at least 1 day.
- For the month preceding the application month, the entrepreneur must have paid the contributions they want to be exempted from. _(i.e. you can't game the system and have the state pay your voluntary składka chorobowa during the vacation if you weren't paying it yourself before that)_
- Have no more than 9 employees.
- Annual turnover below 2 million euros.
- The entrepreneur did not provide services to a former employer for whom, in the year of business registration and the year prior, they worked under an employment contract (w ramach stosunku pracy) or another contract (spółdzielczego stosunku pracy), in the same field as their current business activity.
- Has not already used ZUS vacation this year.

## Which Contributions Are Exempt

ZUS vacation exempts you from paying social contributions:

- mandatory składki społeczne for yourself: emerytalne, rentowe i wypadkowe;
- voluntary ubezpieczenie chorobowe;
- contributions to the Fundusz Pracy and Fundusz Solidarnościowy.

In terms of numbers, the ZUS vacation savings work out the same as the "Ulga na Start" benefit. Keep in mind that the health insurance contribution ("składka zdrowotna") must be paid regardless.

## How to Apply for ZUS Vacation

You can only apply **online** through the PUE ZUS / eZUS portal.
The original version of this guide was developed by [:telegram: Olga Korneva][18].

### Creating the Document

Go to the Płatnik panel (top right), then select - Dokumenty i wiadomości
(left column) - Dokumenty robocze, and at the bottom click Utwórz nowy.

![Create document][1]

### Selecting RWS

At this step, you need to find the right application form. First, increase the number of
documents per page (at the bottom, select 50 or 100) and scroll to find the form, or page
through and look for: Wniosek RWS - Wniosek o zwolnienie z obowiązku opłacenia składek za wskazany miesiąc.
Select it and click Utwórz dokument at the bottom.

![Choose RWS][2]

### Selecting the Type

Select the document type Wniosek.

* [ ] Withdrawal of the application

Click Dalej to proceed to the next screen.

### Declaring the Use of De Minimis Aid

ZUS vacation is a form of de minimis aid, which means the entrepreneur must declare all
government subsidies and business benefits received during the current year.
The total de minimis aid limit is 300,000 euros over three years.

![De minimis][22]

!!! quote
    I declare that:

    - for the last two calendar years preceding the year of application, I did not receive income from non-agricultural economic activity, or in at least one year of the last two calendar years preceding the year of application, I received annual income from non-agricultural economic activity that did not exceed the equivalent in zlotys of 2 million euros;
    - as an insured person in the calendar year preceding the year of application and from the beginning of the calendar year in which I submit the application until the date of its submission, I did not conduct non-agricultural business activity for a former employer for whom, in the calendar year of starting business activity or in the previous calendar year, I performed, within the framework of employment or cooperative employment relationships, activities falling within the scope of the business activity;
    - Over the past 3 years:
         - [ ] I did not use de minimis state aid
         - [x] I used de minimis public aid
         To confirm the amount of state aid received, I am providing:
         - [ ] a declaration
         - [x] certificates

     :warning: Before submitting the application, attach the certificates as appendices.
     You will be able to do this on the last page of the creator.

     **I am aware of criminal liability for making a false declaration.**

In the example, the option with using state aid is checked — specifically, using
ZUS vacation in the previous calendar year. Select the option that applies to you.

#### FAQ

A common question from people who aren't sure: _"what if I received de minimis aid and just don't know?"_

You <u>will</u> know if you received such aid: the entire amount must be strictly accounted for
to avoid exceeding the three-year threshold, so any body providing such aid
is required to issue a certificate of aid with a specific date and amount. Additionally,
there is a [register of entrepreneurs][19] who received aid, where you can look yourself up.
Enter your NIP, click the button to select the time range pełne 3 lata wstecz,
check the field tylko de minimis, and then click Wyszukaj według wybranych kryteriów.
A file will be generated listing all individually received benefits along with their total sum.

_"Where do I get the de minimis aid certificate?"_

In PUE ZUS, panel Platnik - Dokumenty i wiadomości - Skrzynka odbiorcza. In the list of received documents, find all relevant documents named RWS - Informacja o przyznaniu zwolnienia. For each such document, go to Szczegóły.

![Skrzynka odbiorcza][53]

On the Szczegóły panel, in the Załączniki section, look for the document _Zaświadczenie_rws-r-zasw.pdf_. To download the document, click Pobierz. Adding documents is described in the last step of filling out the application.

### Entrepreneur Categories

![Categories][23]

Translation of the content with category explanations
!!! quote "Translation"
    Entity size

    The size of the organization in accordance with [Annex I to Commission Regulation (EU) No 651/2014 of June 17, 2014](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/PL/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32014R0651), recognizing certain types of aid as compatible with the internal market in accordance with Articles [107](https://sip.lex.pl/akty-prawne/dzu-dziennik-ustaw/traktat-o-funkcjonowaniu-unii-europejskiej-rzym-1957-03-25-17099384/art-107) and [108](https://sip.lex.pl/akty-prawne/dzu-dziennik-ustaw/traktat-o-funkcjonowaniu-unii-europejskiej-rzym-1957-03-25-17099384/art-108) of the Treaty (regulation).

**Micro entrepreneur** — an entrepreneur who, for at least one of the last two financial years, cumulatively met the following conditions:

- had an average of fewer than 10 employees;
- achieved net annual turnover from the sale of goods, products, and services, as well as from financial operations, not exceeding the equivalent in zlotys of 2 million euros, or the total assets on their balance sheet prepared at the end of one of those years did not exceed the equivalent in zlotys of 2 million euros.

**Small entrepreneur** — who, for at least one of the last two financial years, cumulatively met the following conditions:

- had an average of fewer than 50 employees;
- achieved net annual turnover from the sale of goods, products, and services, as well as from financial operations, not exceeding the equivalent in zlotys of 10 million euros, or the total assets on their balance sheet prepared at the end of one of those years did not exceed the equivalent in zlotys of 10 million euros, and — is not a micro entrepreneur.

**Medium entrepreneur** — who, for at least one of the last two financial years, cumulatively met the following conditions:

- had an average of fewer than 250 employees;
- achieved net annual turnover from the sale of goods, products, and services, as well as from financial operations, not exceeding the equivalent in zlotys of 50 million euros, or the total assets on their balance sheet prepared at the end of one of those years did not exceed the equivalent in zlotys of 43 million euros, and — is not a micro entrepreneur or a small entrepreneur.

**Other entrepreneur** — who does not fall into any of the above categories.

In the example, **Micro entrepreneur** is selected. This is the only correct answer in the sense that if you have 10 or more employees, ZUS vacation is not for you.
Select the option that applies to you.

### What's Next

Wait for a response from ZUS in your PUE/eZUS account. This may take several days.
You will receive a notification about new messages at the email address or phone number
you provided in PUE/eZUS.

First, you will receive Zawiadomienie o możliwości czynnego udziału w sprawie — this is an informational letter from ZUS that your application has been accepted for review. After that, within a few days, you will receive either a positive or negative decision about participating in ZUS vacation. In the case of a positive decision, the document is called Informacja o przyznaniu zwolnienia. The example shows a case where the vacation application was approved.

![Recieved documents][55]

If you don't pay the voluntary składka chorobowa (the one for sick leave) and apply for the vacation, ZUS may at some point send something like this:

!!!quote "Zawiadomenie"
    Zakończyliśmy postępowanie w sprawie ustalenia prawa do zwolnienia z obowiązku opłacenia składek za czerwiec 2025 r., wszczętego na Pana wniosek z 29 maja 2025 r.

    W wyniku weryfikacji stwierdziliśmy, że nie spełnia Pan następujących przesłanek ustawowych:

    • nie podlegał Pan dobrowolnemu ubezpieczeniu chorobowemu w miesiącu złożenia wniosku albo w miesiącu go poprzedzającym

    Może Pan wypowiedzieć się o zgromadzonej w Zakładzie dokumentacji w terminie 7 dni od daty doręczenia tego zawiadomienia.

Don't panic — this does not mean a full rejection of the vacation, just a denial of the składka chorobowa compensation. A few days after this, you'll receive the normal documents regarding the aid.

## Monthly Reports During ZUS Vacation

The entrepreneur must submit the following documents for themselves:

- **ZUS RCA 1**: a personal social insurance report (składka na ubezpieczenie społeczne) **accounting for the vacation**. The report must specify the **appropriate Kod tytułu ubezpieczenia - 0514 for Duży ZUS, 0574 for Składki preferencyjne, 0594 for Mały ZUS Plus.**
- **ZUS RCA 2**: a personal health insurance report (składka na ubezpieczenie zdrowotne) and, if applicable, voluntary sick leave contributions (dobrowolne ubezpieczenie chorobowe) **without accounting for the vacation**. The report must specify the **current Kod tytułu ubezpieczenia - 0510 for Duży ZUS, 0570 for Składki preferencyjne, 0590 for Mały ZUS Plus.**
- **ZUS DRA**: a contributions declaration **accounting for the vacation**. In the declaration, in the social insurance payment sections (**Składki finansowane przez** sections), the values should be entered in the _budżet państwa_ fields instead of _ubezpieczonych_.

The ZUS vacation benefit does not apply to employees.
If the entrepreneur has employees, they must submit the following documents for them:

- **ZUS RCA**: a personal report **without accounting for the vacation**.

## You Don't Lose Anything

The state will pay your contributions for that month — this has no effect on your pension savings.
Unlike a "pause" (zawieszenie) or sick leave, during ZUS vacation the entrepreneur can
continue conducting income-generating activity and issuing invoices.

## Conclusion

ZUS vacation is a great opportunity to reduce business expenses a bit and make your life easier.
Just don't forget to carefully review the eligibility criteria and submit your application on time.
